Our Dining Philosophy

Simple Food,
Served Honestly

We don't believe a resort meal needs to be complicated to be memorable. At Lemon Leaf, dinner means a full buffet spread of vegetarian and non-vegetarian dishes, served warm, eaten outdoors, usually with a bonfire crackling somewhere nearby.

From your welcome drink at check-in to the last cup of chai by the fire, food here is less about plating and more about the company you're sharing the table with.
