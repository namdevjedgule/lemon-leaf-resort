About Lemon Leaf Resort

Pune's Finest
Luxury Glamping Escape

Nestled in the picturesque village of Wadhane, Bhor — Lemon Leaf Resort is where nature and luxury meet. Wake up to misty mountain views, take a dip in our infinity pool overlooking the shimmering lake, and spend your evenings under the open sky.

Strategically located near the historic Rajgad Fort, Torna Fort, Necklace Point & Bhatghar Dam, we are the ideal base for adventure and relaxation alike — just 55 km from Pune Railway Station.

  • Open Sky Theatre & Bonfire every night

Common Questions

Everything You
Need to Know

Check-in is from 2:00 PM and check-out is by 11:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out can be arranged subject to availability - please contact us at the time of booking.

All stays include a complimentary welcome bonfire with snacks and a farm-fresh breakfast. Lunch and dinner are available at an additional cost through our in-resort dining.

Children above 5 years are welcome. We have special kids' activity programmes on weekends. Unfortunately, we currently do not accommodate pets to protect the forest wildlife.

Cancellations made 7+ days before check-in receive a full refund. Within 3–7 days: 50% refund. Within 72 hours: no refund. We strongly recommend travel insurance for last-minute changes.

We offer limited Wi-Fi in the common lounge area. Individual stays are intentionally kept offline - we believe disconnecting is part of the healing. Mobile signals are available for most networks.

We offer a pickup service from Nashik city at ₹800 per trip (one-way). Please book this in advance. Trains and buses to Nashik are well-connected from Mumbai and Pune.
